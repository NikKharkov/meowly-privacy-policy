Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20.08.2025
Last Updated: 20.08.2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Meowly ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address for account creation and authentication
  • User Data: Information you choose to save within the app for your personal use
  • Technical Information: Device information and app usage data for functionality and improvement purposes
  • Advertising ID: We may collect your device's advertising identifier for analytics and app improvement purposes

2. Permissions We Use

Our app requests the following permissions to provide you with the best experience:

  • Internet Access: Required for account authentication, data synchronization, and app functionality
  • Storage Access: Used to save your data locally on your device (only on older Android versions)
  • Notifications: Allows the app to send you important updates and reminders
  • Vibration: Provides haptic feedback for better user experience
  • Advertising ID: Used for analytics to improve app performance and functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing your user account
  • Authenticating your access to the app
  • Saving and syncing your personal data across devices
  • Providing customer support when needed
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Sending you important notifications about the app
  • Analyzing app usage to enhance performance

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data remains private and is used exclusively for the app's functionality.

The only exception is our use of Firebase Authentication by Google for secure account management. Google may process your authentication data according to their privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Authentication: Managed by Google with industry-standard security measures
  • Encrypted Storage: Your personal data is encrypted and stored securely
  • Local Storage: Some data may be stored locally on your device for offline access
  • Access Controls: Only you can access your personal information through your authenticated account

6. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase Authentication provided by Google Inc. for secure user authentication. Google's handling of your authentication data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide you with our services. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Opt out of notifications at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. International Users

This app complies with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European users and other regional privacy regulations.
